How It Works

Galvanize Café

Everyone has their favorite seat to be most productive or creative.

Its easy with Galvanize App where you can make a seat reservation right from your mobile phone.

Mobile Ordering

No need to stop working or stand in long lines. Simply use the Galvanize App to make a purchase.

Frictionless Check-In

Recieve deals and promotions when you check-in with Galvanize Beacon or Scanning QR Code.

Galvanize Beacon will notify you to check-out when you leave the Café.

Special Order Request

Need a food recommendation? Have a design for latte art you wish to convey?

Galvanize App allows you to get recommendations through real-time livechat. It even features an interactive interface for you to draw a latte art design on your favorite beverage.

Community & Social

Share about your favorite orders with the rest of the community and make new friends!
